Attending ALTA

Registration

All attendees must register before the event via trybooking.com (free for students and AUD80 for academics and professionals). Registration is now open.

Venue

ALTA 2017 will be held in Room P419 (P Block) and Room 421 (P Block) at the Gardens Point Campus, Queensland University of Technology in Brisbane. Please refer to the Gardens Point campus map for directions to the conference venue.

Accomodation

You may find booking.com useful for booking the accomoodation.

Dinner

The dinner is included in the registration fee for all participants (including students). The dinner will be held from 7pm at the Korilla BBQ Restaurant (1/43 Queen St, Brisbane City). This is within the Brisbane Queen St Mall area.

Student Travel Support

ALTA will be offering travel support for students to attend and present at ALTA 2017 in Brisbane. The number of scholarships available, and their value, are still to be determined. These awards can be used to reimburse some of the travel and accommodation expenses. The organising committee reserves the right to make awards as they see fit, or to alter the amount of any awards.

Selected students for the awards will be contacted by the organising committee by mid November 2017. Preference will be given to students who will be presenting a paper at the workshop.
